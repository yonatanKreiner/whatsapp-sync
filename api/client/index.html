WhatsAppSync



Synchronize your phone contacts with WhatsApp photos

WhatsAppSync is a simple solution for adding photos to phone contacts.

Step 1

Open WhatsApp on your phone. Click on the options menu (3 dots on top), and choose "WhatsApp Web" option.
Scan the QR code on this website.

Step 2

Click the import button below the QR code and connect with your Google account.
Allow WhatsAppSync to access your Google contacts by clicking authorize after authenticating to Google.
